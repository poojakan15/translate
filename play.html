About


Hi there! I’m Pooja Kanuri and welcome to my website. This all started because my friends and I would try to translate a word in a different language just for fun. Being stuck at home during quarantine and with boredom killing me, I figured I’d make a game out of it. If you have any questions/comments, reach out to me via the Contact page. Now, let’s play the game and find out how strong your language skills are..


* This game is available as a web application and is currently not available as a mobile app. It currently includes Telugu and Hindi because those are the languages I know. Might add more later...stay tuned and have fun translating!
